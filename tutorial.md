# [Tutorial] Getting NIGHT Tokens: Exchanges, Bridging & Wallet Funding on Mainnet

## Introduction
The Midnight Network is a data-protection blockchain that safeguards sensitive commercial and personal data. To interact with the network, deploy smart contracts, or execute shielded transactions, you need **NIGHT tokens**. This guide will walk you through the current methods for acquiring NIGHT, bridging them, and funding your wallet.

## Step 1: Setting Up Your Wallet
Before acquiring tokens, you need a compatible wallet.
1. Download the official Midnight Wallet extension.
2. Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase.
3. Switch the network to Mainnet.

## Step 2: Acquiring NIGHT Tokens
Currently, NIGHT tokens can be acquired through:
- **Supported Exchanges:** Check the official Midnight Network announcements for listed CEXs/DEXs.
- **Bridging:** If you have assets on Cardano (ADA), you may use the official bridge (once live) to swap for NIGHT.

## Step 3: Funding Your Wallet
1. Copy your Midnight Wallet address.
2. Withdraw NIGHT from the exchange to your wallet address.
3. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain.

## Conclusion
You are now ready to interact with the Midnight Network! Always ensure you are using official links and double-check addresses before sending funds.
