# [Tutorial] Getting NIGHT Tokens: Exchanges, Bridging & Wallet Funding on Mainnet

## Introduction
The Midnight Network is a data-protection blockchain that safeguards sensitive commercial and personal data. To interact with the network, deploy smart contracts, or execute shielded transactions, you need **NIGHT tokens**. This guide will walk you through the current methods for acquiring NIGHT.

## Step 1: Acquiring NIGHT on Exchanges
Currently, NIGHT tokens can be acquired through supported centralized and decentralized exchanges.
1. Create an account on a supported exchange.
2. Deposit USDT, ADA, or other supported base pairs.
3. Trade for NIGHT tokens.

## Step 2: Setting up your Midnight Wallet
To hold NIGHT tokens, you need a compatible wallet.
- Download the official Midnight Lace wallet extension.
- Securely store your seed phrase.
- Copy your receiving address.

## Step 3: Bridging and Funding
If you are moving assets from Cardano or other networks:
1. Use the official Midnight Bridge.
2. Connect your Cardano wallet and Midnight wallet.
3. Authorize the transfer.

## Conclusion
With your wallet funded, you are now ready to deploy shielded smart contracts and participate in the Midnight ecosystem!
